BusTracker V1.0

Description:
============
Bus tracker in Arduino for Saskatoon transit. It will check the waiting time on a desired stop and display it on a 7-segment display.


HARDWARE:
=========
Arduino Uno
Adafruit CC3000 WiFi shield
Adafruit 1.2" 7-segment red
Buttons


SOFTWARE:
=========
Arduino IDE 1.6.8


MODULES:
========


LIBRARIES:
==========
Wire
Adafruit_GFX (https://github.com/adafruit/Adafruit-GFX-Library)
Adafruit LED Backpack (https://github.com/adafruit/Adafruit_LED_Backpack)
Adafruit CC3000 (https://github.com/adafruit/Adafruit_CC3000_Library)
